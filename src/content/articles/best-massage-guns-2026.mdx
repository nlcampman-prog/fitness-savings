## How Percussive Therapy Works

Percussive therapy works by sending rapid pulses of pressure deep into the muscle tissue. This serves several purposes:

- **Increases Blood Flow:** Moves oxygenated blood to the muscles to speed up repair.
- **Reduces Muscle Soreness (DOMS):** Helps flush out metabolic waste and lactic acid.
- **Relieves Tightness:** Breaks up adhesions and improves range of motion.

## What to Look for in a Massage Gun

1. **Amplitude:** How deep the head travels (usually measured in mm). 16mm is considered high-end.
2. **Stall Force:** How much pressure you can apply before the motor stops.
3. **Battery Life:** Look for at least 2-3 hours of continuous use.
4. **Noise Level:** A quiet motor makes for a much more pleasant recovery session.

**Safety Tip:** Avoid using a massage gun directly on bones, joints, or your neck. Focus on large muscle groups like quads, hamstrings, and glutes.
