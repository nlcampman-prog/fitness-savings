## Why Creatine is a Must-Have

Creatine works by helping your muscles produce more ATP, the primary molecule your cells use for energy. During high-intensity exercise, your body burns ATP faster than it can replenish it. Creatine bridges that gap.

- **Improved Strength:** Increases explosive power and max lifts.
- **Faster Recovery:** Reduces muscle cell damage and inflammation.
- **Brain Health:** New research in 2026 suggests creatine may improve memory and mental clarity under stress.

## Monohydrate vs. HCL: Which is the Better Deal?

In 2026, the debate continues, but the consensus remains: **Creatine Monohydrate** is the gold standard for value. It is the most researched form and generally the most affordable. **Creatine HCL** is more soluble and may cause less bloating for some, but it usually comes with a higher price tag per serving.
