## Sample Full-Body Routine (Mat + Dumbbells + Bands)

1. **Goblet Squats (Dumbbells):** 3 sets of 12 reps
2. **Push-Ups (Optional Banded):** 3 sets to failure
3. **Dumbbell Rows:** 3 sets of 10 reps per side
4. **Glute Bridges (Resistance Band):** 3 sets of 15 reps
5. **Overhead Press (Dumbbells):** 3 sets of 10 reps
6. **Plank (Yoga Mat):** 3 sets of 45 seconds

## Tips for Staying Motivated as a Beginner

- **Designate a Space:** Even if it's just a 6x6 area, having a "gym zone" helps flip the mental switch into workout mode.
- **Track Your Progress:** Use a simple notebook or a free app to record your reps and weights. Seeing the numbers go up is the best motivator.
- **Start Small:** Aim for 20-30 minutes three times a week. Consistency is more important than intensity when you're just starting out.
