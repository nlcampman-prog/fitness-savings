## What to Look for in a Pre-Workout

Before we dive into the list, it's important to understand the key ingredients that actually drive performance:

- **Caffeine:** The gold standard for energy and focus. Most effective doses range from 150mg to 350mg.
- **L-Citrulline:** An amino acid that boosts nitric oxide levels, leading to better blood flow and "muscle pumps." Look for 6g-8g of pure L-Citrulline or 8g-10g of Citrulline Malate.
- **Beta-Alanine:** Known for the "tingles," this helps buffer lactic acid, allowing you to squeeze out those last few reps. Doses should be between 3.2g and 6.4g.
- **L-Tyrosine:** A nootropic that supports cognitive function and focus under stress.

## Safety and Third-Party Testing

When choosing a supplement, always look for third-party certifications like **NSF Certified for Sport** or **Informed Choice**. These labels ensure that what is on the bottle is actually in the tub and that the product is free from banned substances.

**Usage Tip:** Start with a half-scoop to assess your tolerance, especially with high-stimulant formulas like Legion Pulse or Gorilla Mode.
