## Why Use Mushroom Coffee as a Pre-Workout?

Traditional pre-workouts often contain upwards of 300mg of caffeine anhydrous (a highly processed, fast-acting stimulant) alongside artificial sweeteners, dyes, and synthetic vasodilators. While they "wake you up," they also trigger cortisol spikes, stress the adrenal glands, and disrupt sleep.

Mushroom coffee takes a completely different approach to energy. Here is why it has become the preferred pre-workout and morning daily driver for fitness-conscious women:

1. **Jitter-Free Clean Energy:** By pairing functional mushrooms with organic, lower-dose caffeine, you get a smooth rise in energy. Functional mushrooms slow down the absorption of caffeine, leading to a prolonged release of energy rather than a sharp spike and crash.
2. **Cordyceps for Oxygen Utilization:** Cordyceps militaris is famous in sports science for increasing VO2 max (maximum oxygen uptake) and ATP (cellular energy) production. This translates directly into better endurance, letting you power through runs, HIIT sessions, or heavy lifting sets without feeling winded.
3. **Lion's Mane for Mind-Muscle Connection:** Lion's Mane is a powerful nootropic that stimulates Nerve Growth Factor (NGF). In a workout context, this enhances the "mind-muscle connection," helping you stay locked into your form and training goals.
4. **Adrenal and Stress Support:** Adaptogens like Reishi, Chaga, and Ashwagandha help regulate your body's stress response. This means your workouts stimulate fat adaptation and fitness progression rather than exhausting your nervous system.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does mushroom coffee taste like mushrooms?
No! High-quality brands use extraction processes that isolate the active compounds of the mushrooms while neutralizing their savory flavors. The final product tastes like premium, earthy coffee, often with pleasant notes of cacao or tea.

### Can pregnant or nursing mothers drink mushroom coffee?
Many moms prefer mushroom coffee because of its lower caffeine content. However, because adaptogenic herbs can affect hormone levels, it is always best to consult with your healthcare provider before introducing them to your routine during pregnancy or postpartum.

### Will mushroom coffee break an intermittent fast?
If consumed black (mixed only with hot or cold water), RYZE and MUD/WTR contain negligible calories (under 10-15 per serving) and will not break your metabolic fast. Everyday Dose contains a small amount of collagen protein, which may trigger a very mild digestive response, but is still considered excellent for fat-burning fasted states.
