## The Science: How Red Light Therapy Works

Red light therapy works by emitting low-level wavelengths of red and near-infrared (NIR) light. Unlike the light from your phone or the sun's UV rays, these wavelengths penetrate deep into the skin and into the mitochondria—the powerhouse of your cells.

- **ATP Production:** RLT stimulates the mitochondria to produce more Adenosine Triphosphate (ATP), providing the energy needed for cellular repair.
- **Reduced Inflammation:** It helps lower oxidative stress and reduces the presence of pro-inflammatory cytokines.
- **Increased Collagen:** Supports the repair of connective tissues and skin health.

## Benefits for Muscle Recovery

Using red light therapy before or after a workout can lead to:

1. **Faster Recovery Times:** Reduced muscle soreness (DOMS) after intense training.
2. **Improved Muscle Endurance:** Some studies in 2026 show that RLT can increase the number of reps an athlete can perform before fatigue.
3. **Injury Prevention:** Strengthening the cellular matrix of the muscles and tendons.
