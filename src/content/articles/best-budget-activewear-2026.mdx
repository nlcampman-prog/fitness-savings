## How to Make Your Budget Gear Last

To ensure your affordable activewear stands the test of time, follow these 2026 care tips:

1. **Wash Cold:** High heat damages the elastic fibers (spandex/elastane).
2. **Skip the Fabric Softener:** It coats the fibers and ruins the "moisture-wicking" properties of the fabric.
3. **Air Dry Only:** Dryers are the #1 killer of activewear. Hang your gear to dry to keep the shape and stretch intact.
