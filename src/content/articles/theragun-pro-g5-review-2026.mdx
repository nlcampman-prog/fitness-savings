## What’s New in the 5th Generation?

The G5 isn't just a minor spec bump; it introduces several significant improvements over the previous PRO model:

### 1. 20% Quieter Motor
The biggest complaint about earlier Theraguns was the noise—they sounded like a construction site. The G5 features a redesigned proprietary brushless motor with **QuietForce Technology™** that is significantly quieter without sacrificing an ounce of power.

### 2. High-Res OLED Screen
The new built-in screen provides visual feedback on your force levels and battery life, and even guides you through built-in recovery routines. You no longer need to check your phone to know if you're pressing too hard.

### 3. New EQ (Equalizer) Mode
The G5 allows you to customize the speed and force settings more granularly than ever before, ensuring you get the exact intensity needed for different muscle groups.

## Ease of Use and Ergonomics

One of the Theragun's greatest strengths is the **Patented Triangle Handle**. It allows you to hold the device in multiple ways, making it easy to reach 80% of your body—including your own lower back—without straining your wrist.

The G5 also comes with **6 high-quality attachments**, including the "Supersoft" head for sensitive areas and the "Wedge" for shoulder blades and IT bands.

## The App Integration

The Therabody App is arguably the best in the business. It connects to your device via Bluetooth and syncs with your Apple Health or Strava data. If you just finished a long run, the app will suggest a specific "Runner's Recovery" routine and automatically control the speed of your Theragun while you follow along.
