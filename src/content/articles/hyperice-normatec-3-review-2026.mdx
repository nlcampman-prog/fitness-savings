## What is Dynamic Air Compression?

Normatec uses a patented technology called **Pulse Massage**. Unlike static compression (like compression socks), dynamic compression uses compressed air to massage your limbs, mobilize fluid, and speed up recovery.

The boots feature five overlapping zones that inflate sequentially to mimic the natural muscle pump of the body, pushing metabolic waste out of the limbs and inviting fresh, oxygenated blood back in.

## Key Features of the Normatec 3

The 3rd generation of Normatec introduced several quality-of-life updates that make it the market leader in 2026:

### 1. The Redesigned Control Unit
The control unit is now smaller, lighter, and features an intuitive interface. It’s battery-operated, meaning you aren't tethered to a wall outlet while you recover. You can take them to the track, the gym, or use them on your couch.

### 2. 7 Intensity Levels and Zone Boost
Whether you want a light "flush" after a walk or a high-intensity session after a marathon, the 7 levels of pressure have you covered. The **Zone Boost** feature allows you to add extra time and pressure to a specific area (like your calves or quads) that needs extra love.

### 3. Hyperice App Integration
Bluetooth connectivity allows you to control the session from your phone. The app offers specialized routines based on your activity, whether it's cycling, running, or even "standing all day."
