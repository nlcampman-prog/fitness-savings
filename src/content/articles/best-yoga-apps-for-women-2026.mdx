## What to Look For in a Home Yoga App

Before downloading a subscription app, ensure it meets these three critical standards:

1.  **Time and Intensity Customization:** Your app should allow you to filter classes by duration (from 5 to 60+ minutes) and physical intensity (from restorative stretching to sweaty, athletic vinyasa flows).
2.  **Smart Personalization:** Look for apps that actively design custom-tailored multi-week challenge programs based on your specific physical goals, whether that is weight loss, pelvic floor recovery, core toning, or stress relief.
3.  **Clear Audiovisual Instruction:** Since you are practicing at home, high-definition video and clear, precise vocal cues are essential so you can transition between poses without constantly craning your neck to look at your device's screen.
