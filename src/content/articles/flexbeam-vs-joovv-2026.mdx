## The Fundamentals: How They Differ

Before diving into the brands, it’s important to understand the two different ways to deliver red and near-infrared light.

### FlexBeam: The Targeted Approach
FlexBeam is a wearable device designed to be strapped directly onto the body. Its ergonomic, curved design allows the light to wrap around joints (like knees or shoulders) or sit directly on muscle groups (like the lower back or quads). 

Because the light source is in direct contact with the skin, there is minimal "light spill," meaning more of the energy is delivered exactly where you need it.

### Joovv: The Whole-Body Approach
Joovv is the pioneer of high-quality RLT panels. These are typically mounted on a wall or a stand. You stand or sit in front of the panel for a set amount of time (usually 10-20 minutes). 

Joovv panels are designed to cover large areas of the body—or even the entire body if you have a multi-panel setup. This is ideal for systemic benefits like hormonal support, skin health over large areas, and overall mood enhancement.

## Portability vs. Coverage: The Ultimate Trade-off

The decision ultimately comes down to your primary use case. 

If you have a **specific injury** or a recurring "problem area" like a tight lower back, the FlexBeam’s ability to deliver targeted energy directly to the tissue is superior. The light doesn't have to travel through the air, losing intensity before it hits your skin.

However, if you want to optimize your **entire body's cellular function**, a Joovv panel provides a "light bath" that a wearable simply can't match.

## Final Verdict 2026

In 2026, many serious biohackers actually use **both**. They use a Joovv panel in the morning for mood and skin health, and a FlexBeam in the evening to target specific sore muscles from their workout.

If you have to choose one:
- **Buy FlexBeam** if you value portability and have specific areas of pain or tension.
- **Buy Joovv** if you want the most comprehensive "whole-person" benefits and have the space for a permanent setup.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is red light therapy safe?
Yes, RLT is a non-invasive, drug-free therapy. Both FlexBeam and Joovv use wavelengths that are well-studied and safe for home use.

### How often should I use it?
For best results, consistency is key. Most users see the best benefits from 10-20 minute sessions, 3-5 times per week.

### Can I use these for skin health?
Absolutely. Both devices are excellent for stimulating collagen production, though Joovv is more efficient for treating large areas like the entire face or back at once.
