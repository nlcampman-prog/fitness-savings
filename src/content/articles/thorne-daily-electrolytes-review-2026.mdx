When you sweat, you lose more than just liquid. You lose essential minerals—sodium, potassium, magnesium, and calcium—that are responsible for nerve conduction and muscle contraction. 

A deficiency in these "spark plugs" of the body leads to:
*   Muscle cramping and spasms.
*   Decreased focus and mental fatigue.
*   Reduced endurance and power output.
*   Slowed recovery times post-workout.
