## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is DOMS, and can supplements cure it?
DOMS stands for Delayed Onset Muscle Soreness, which typically peaks 24 to 48 hours after a new or intense workout. While supplements cannot completely "cure" DOMS, a combination of rapid post-workout protein (1st Phorm) and anti-inflammatory sleep support (Beam Dream) can reduce its severity and duration by up to 50%.

### Can I take collagen instead of protein powder?
No. Collagen is an incomplete protein because it lacks one of the nine essential amino acids (tryptophan). While it is outstanding for structural tissues like joints, skin, and gut, you still need a complete protein source (like 1st Phorm Whey or Equip Beef Isolate) to repair and build muscle tissue.
