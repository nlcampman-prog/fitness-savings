## Category 1: Ingredient Profiles & Sourcing

Let's look under the hood at what you are actually putting into your body.

### RYZE Ingredients:
RYZE keeps things incredibly simple. It contains:
1.  **Organic Mushroom Blend (2,000mg per serving):** Cordyceps, Lion's Mane, Reishi, Chaga, Shiitake, and Turkey Tail.
2.  **Organic Instant Arabica Coffee**
3.  **Organic MCT Oil & Organic Coconut Milk Powder:** Added in very small amounts to provide a smooth texture.

**Key Sourcing Fact:** RYZE uses 100% organic mushrooms grown in the USA. They utilize both the mycelium (root system) and fruiting bodies of the mushrooms grown on organic oats.

### Everyday Dose Ingredients:
Everyday Dose has a more complex, multi-functional ingredient list:
1.  **Organic Double-Extracted Mushroom Blend:** Lion's Mane and Chaga.
2.  **Premium Grass-Fed Bovine Collagen (Type I & III):** Essential for joint structural integrity, gut lining repair, and skin elasticity.
3.  **L-Theanine:** An amino acid that promotes a state of calm, relaxed alertness.
4.  **Extract of Cold-Brewed Arabica Coffee:** Low-acid and highly filtered.

**Key Sourcing Fact:** Everyday Dose uses **100% organic fruiting bodies** (the actual "mushroom" cap and stem) rather than mycelium grown on grains. Their double-extraction process (using both hot water and alcohol) ensures maximum bioavailability of beta-glucans.

**Winner: Everyday Dose (for potency and purity); RYZE (for mushroom diversity).**
If you want the maximum variety of functional adaptogens, RYZE's six-mushroom blend is phenomenal. However, for sheer clinical potency, Everyday Dose's use of 100% fruiting bodies with hot water/alcohol extraction, plus the addition of L-Theanine and collagen, makes it the more therapeutically active blend.

**Winner: Tie.**
Both brands land in the "sweet spot" for active women. Everyday Dose's addition of L-Theanine does give it a slight edge in eliminating the "micro-jitters" for highly caffeine-sensitive individuals.
