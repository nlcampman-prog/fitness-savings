## 2. No-Bake High-Protein Energy Bites

If you love meal-prepping, taking 15 minutes on Sunday to roll a batch of high-protein energy bites will set you up for success all week. They are highly portable, toddler-approved, and satisfy your sweet tooth naturally.

### The Recipe:
*   1 cup organic rolled oats
*   1/2 cup organic almond butter or peanut butter
*   1/3 cup raw honey or maple syrup
*   2 scoops of **Equip Chocolate Prime Protein** or **Sprout Living Vanilla Epic Protein**
*   2 tbsp chia seeds or ground flaxseed
*   A handful of organic dark chocolate chips or unsweetened coconut flakes

### Instructions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl, roll into bite-sized balls, and store in a glass container in your refrigerator. Pop 2 or 3 bites immediately after your workout for a perfect balance of carbs, healthy fats, and clean protein!

## 3. High-Protein Greek Yogurt or Coconut Yogurt Bowl

Greek yogurt is an exceptional, budget-friendly source of whole-food protein (containing roughly 15–20g of protein per cup) and is loaded with gut-friendly probiotics. If you are dairy-free, utilize a high-protein coconut-cultured yogurt.

### How to Build Your Bowl:
*   **Base:** 1 cup of unsweetened plain Greek yogurt or organic coconut yogurt.
*   **Carbs/Antioxidants:** 1/2 cup of fresh organic blueberries or raspberries (to fight exercise-induced oxidative stress).
*   **Fiber/Fats:** 1 tablespoon of chia seeds or pumpkin seeds.
*   **The Sweet Touch:** A light drizzle of raw local honey.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What happens if I don't eat after working out?
If you skip post-workout nutrition, your body remains in a catabolic (muscle-breakdown) state. This can lead to increased muscle soreness (DOMS), prolonged recovery times, sluggish thyroid/metabolism, elevated cortisol (stress hormone) levels, and intense cravings for sugary foods later in the day.

### Can I share these snacks with my kids?
Absolutely! The high-protein energy bites, Greek yogurt bowls, and avocado egg toast are incredibly nutritious whole foods that are excellent for growing children. Even the clean protein powders like **Equip Prime Protein** and **Sprout Living Epic Protein** are made with pure, whole-food ingredients and are completely safe for kids.
