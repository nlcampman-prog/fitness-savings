## What is the AEKE Digital Fitness Mirror?

AEKE is a high-tech fitness brand that focuses on "integrated smart fitness." Unlike traditional equipment that relies on heavy iron plates, AEKE utilizes **digital resistance**. This allows for a massive amount of weight capacity in a device that is roughly the size of a standard full-length mirror.

The AEKE mirror is designed to be "invisible" when not in use, blending seamlessly into modern home decor. But don't let the sleek aesthetics fool you; underneath the glass and aluminum is a motor-driven resistance system capable of challenging even seasoned lifters.

## Key Features for 2026

### 1. Digital Weight Technology
The magic of AEKE lies in its electromagnetic motors. You can change your "weight" with a tap on the screen, switching from 5 lbs for lateral raises to 200 lbs for squats in seconds. Because it’s digital, the resistance is constant throughout the entire range of motion—there’s no momentum to help you "cheat" the rep.

### 2. Real-Time AI Coaching
In 2026, the AEKE mirror features an advanced computer vision system. Using an integrated 4K camera, the AI analyzes your form in real-time, providing audio and visual cues if your back is rounding or if your squat depth isn't sufficient. It’s like having a personal trainer standing in the room with you.

### 3. Compact Footprint
The AEKE mirror occupies zero floor space if wall-mounted, and less than 3 square feet if used with its floor stand. For apartment dwellers, this is the ultimate "gym in a box."
