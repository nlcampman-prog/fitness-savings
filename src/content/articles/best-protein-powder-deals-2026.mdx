## Quality vs. Price: How to Spot a Good Deal

When shopping for protein, don't just look at the total price. A cheap tub that is half-full of fillers is not a deal. Always consider:

- **Protein Percentage:** Total protein per serving divided by total serving size. Aim for 70-80%+.
- **Amino Profile:** Ensure it includes all Essential Amino Acids (EAAs).
- **Sweeteners:** Stevia and Monk Fruit are generally better for gut health than sucralose or aspartame.
