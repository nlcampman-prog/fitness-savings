The onboarding process for Trainwell is remarkably thorough, which is a good sign if you’re looking for a truly personalized experience.

### 1. Match with Your Coach
You start by answering a series of questions about your goals (weight loss, muscle gain, mobility), your equipment (full gym, home gym, or just a pair of dumbbells), and your preferred coaching style. Do you want a "drill sergeant" or a "supportive cheerleader"? Trainwell then suggests a few coaches who fit your profile.

### 2. The Video Consultation
Once matched, you’ll have a video call with your coach. This is where the magic happens. You’ll discuss your history, any injuries, and exactly how fitness fits into your life. In 2026, this level of personal connection is rare in the digital space.

### 3. Your Custom Program
Your coach then builds a program specifically for you. The app features high-quality video demonstrations for every movement, and if you have an Apple Watch or other wearable, it syncs your heart rate and movement data directly to your coach.
